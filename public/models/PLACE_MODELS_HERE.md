## How to Download from Sketchfab

1. Create a free Sketchfab account
2. Go to the model page
3. Click "Download 3D Model"
4. Choose "glTF" or "GLB" format
5. Extract and rename to match the expected filename
6. Place in this folder

## For iOS AR (Optional but Recommended)

For the best iOS AR experience, also convert your GLB to USDZ format:

### Option 1: Online Converter
- https://products.groupdocs.app/conversion/glb-to-usdz

### Option 2: Reality Converter (Mac only)
- Download from Apple: https://developer.apple.com/augmented-reality/tools/
- Open your GLB file
- Export as USDZ

Name your USDZ files to match (e.g., `ark-of-the-covenant.usdz`)
